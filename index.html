About Brunei Beauty

Betta Macrostoma, commonly known as the Brunei Beauty or Spotfin Betta, is a medium-sized species of freshwater fish. Adult specimens typically reach sizes between 2 to 3 inches (5 to 8 centimeters) in length.s

Native to the island of Borneo in Southeast Asia, Betta Macrostoma is specifically found in the Sultanate of Brunei and Malaysian state of Sarawak. This species inhabits slow-moving or stagnant waters in its natural environment.

The breeding process of Betta Macrostoma.

1. Male's Courtship Display

Males perform a courtship display, characterized by erecting fins and bending their bodies, showcasing improved skills as they reach adulthood. The display aims to impress the female and induce spawning behavior.

2. Female's Courtship Display

After a period of dormancy, the female undergoes a significant color change, losing reddish-pink tones and developing horizontal black lines. She performs a dance-like courtship display, signaling her readiness to mate.

3. Spawning Embraces

The actual spawning embraces have a low success rate, with less than 20% resulting in fertilization. Embraces include both practice runs and the release of eggs and sperm, with the male's anal fin acting as a platform for the eggs.

4. Egg Transfer

After successful embraces, the female transfers the eggs to the male, who catches them in his mouth. This process continues until the female exhausts her eggs.

5. Oral Incubation

The male holds the fertilized eggs in his mouth for an incubation period lasting 15-30 days. This stage is crucial and challenging, with many males failing to hold the eggs to term.

6. Release of Fry

If incubation is successful, the male releases fully formed and free-swimming fry. The timing of fry release varies among males, occurring between 17 to 24 days. Fry are larger in size compared to other Betta species.

Additionally, the breeder must consider the female's behavior post-spawning to decide whether she should be removed from the tank. Factors such as the male's experience and the female's personality influence the success of the breeding process. The fishkeeper should be patient and observant during the incubation period, ensuring the male doesn't get spooked, and be cautious about potential issues like the male swallowing the eggs prematurely. After fry release, the tank undergoes a thorough cleaning, and the male is reintroduced to regular feeding gradually. Betta macrostoma are not picky eaters, accepting various foods.

Betta Macrostoma is currently classified as an endangered species, facing threats primarily due to habitat loss and degradation. The conversion of natural habitats for agriculture, logging, and urban development poses a significant risk to the survival of this species. Additionally, pollution and collection for the aquarium trade contribute to the decline in wild populations.

Conservation efforts, including responsible breeding practices and habitat preservation, are crucial for the long-term survival of Betta Macrostoma and its ecosystems. Initiatives such as those undertaken by Macrostoma Aquatics aim to raise awareness and actively contribute to the conservation of this unique and endangered species.

Our mission

The primary objective of our conservation programme is to contribute to the sustainability and well-being of Betta Macrostoma in the aquarium hobby. By engaging experienced fishkeepers, we aim to foster a community dedicated to the careful breeding and nurturing of these magnificent creatures.

How it works

1. Apply for participation

  • Experienced fishkeepers with a passion for Betta Macrostoma are invited to apply for our programme.
  • Submit your application, sharing your expertise and commitment to responsible fishkeeping.

2. Selection Process:

  • Our team carefully reviews applications to ensure that participants align with the principles of ethical fishkeeping and conservation.
  • Selected participants will be notified and provided with detailed guidelines for the programme.

3. Receive Betta Macrostoma Fry

  • Upon acceptance, participants will receive a batch of five Betta Macrostoma fry from Macrostoma Aquatics
  • These fry are sourced from our conservation efforts and come with a genetic lineage aimed at promoting diversity and health.

4. Cultivate Responsible Fishkeeping

  • Participants play a crucial role in the conservation process by raising and caring for the Betta Macrostoma fry.
  • Detailed care instructions and support will be provided by Macrostoma Aquatics to ensure the well-being of the fry.

5. Share Your Journey

  • We encourage participants to document and share their experiences through our community platform.
  • Your journey in nurturing Betta Macrostoma contributes to awareness and educates others on responsible fishkeeping practices.
